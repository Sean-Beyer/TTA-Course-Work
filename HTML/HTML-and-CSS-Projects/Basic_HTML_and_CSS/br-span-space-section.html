kAPP

An android who has no memory of his past life after he woke up in a repurposing center.
He tried to get the Stewards to listen to him but they ingored him as being a malfunctioning unit.
When he was about to give up all hope of reclaiming his past,
he was approached by a man claiming to be the captain of a starship in need of a chief mechanic.
kAPP accepted the offer with a promise of being given time to explore himself. During this time, kAPP was able to tinker with his free time and created a drone with artificial intelligence in the form of an old world creature and was named Murco.
