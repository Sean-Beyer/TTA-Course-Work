    Special Abilities

    Everyone specilizes in something and kAPP is an expert with wrenchs and microspanners of an engine room, as well as a workshop.

    Ability Source Description
    Constructed Race

    Androids are counted as both being biological and technologal being gaining resistances to certain effects unless they also affect constructed beings.
    In addition, androids do not breathe or suffer the normal environmental effects of being in a vacuum.
    Exceptional Vision Race

    Androids have low-light vision and darkvision. As a result, they can see in dim light as if it were normal light, and they can see with no light source at all to a range of 60 feet in black and white only.
    Flat Affect Race

    Androids find emotions confusing and keep them bottled up.
    This make them confusing to understand but is the same for them.
    Upgrade Slot Race

    Androids have a single armor upgrade slot in their bodies.
    Tinkerer's knowledge Theme

    kAPP is a visionary in the field of micro-scale construction, able to elevate the applications of many multifunctional materials to new heights.
    This gives him the knowledge to quickly identify technologal devices and specialize in invention and do-it-yourself technology crafting.
    Custom Rig Class

    kAPP has created a customized toolkit he can use to hack systems and items, interact with Murco over great distances and connect to the infospear with a computer upgrade. It has been configured to take up his upgrade slot and fits perfectly into his battleglove attaching his bandolier and his other attachments in his armor. While using this rig, he always count as having the appropriate tool or basic kit for any Computers or Engineering skill check he attempts. Finally, he can communicate with Murco over an encrypted channel to issue commands to its AI or directly control it at a range of 2,500 feet.
    Artificial Intelligence Class

    While serving on a ship, kAPP tinkered with different parts to make a surveillance drone. He decided to model it after the figure of an animal in a museum depicting a creature long lost to the gap.
    As he laid in the last piece of programming, he thought he saw it blink at him. Then it hooted.
    Affixing a camera, manipulator arms for his feet and a laser pistol, Murco turned into a faithful ally able to turn the tide of any situation.
    Percussive Maintenance Feat

    excel at the tried-and-true repair technique of delivering a solid whack at just the right spot to get malfunctioning technology to work.
